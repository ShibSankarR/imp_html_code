Sophie Your dyslexia coach

Hey there,

I'm Sophie,

your dyslexia coach !

And just like you, I'm a mom, parent of two wonderful dyslexic children, and proud dyslexic myself!
I aim to EMPOWER, to TEACH, and INSPIRE parents like me on their dyslexia journey through life experience, both as a dyslexic and as a mom of dyslexic children, but also through effective training.

After homeschooling my children for several years, after having them back to school in advanced placement classes, after learning how to better support each and every one of them, with all their differences and characters, and after helping them being thriving children, I wanted to help YOU - parents of dyslexic children - to do the same successfully.

As a dyslexia coach and consultant, my greatest mission is to help you – parents of dyslexic children – navigate your dyslexia journey with ease by providing you the tools, strategies and motivation, so that your children can overcome their daily challenges, unlock their true potential and thrive!

Let me ask

you something…

  • Have you ever feel ashamed of your child’s dyslexia, completely guilty, and like nobody could understand what you are going through?
  • Afraid that your child is different, afraid that he will never be able to do things like others, afraid that he might not be successful, afraid of what others might say?
  • Afraid to simply admit that your child is truly himself, that you love him, and that maybe something is just missing?

Maybe you’ve spent too much time listening to other people who don’t know anything about your life. Maybe you’ve spent too much time scrolling on internet without finding any real answer. Maybe you are ready to take the step and find the right solution for your child, for your family, but you are still afraid to make a mistake or to be judged. Or maybe you simply don’t know yet what you really want or where to start.

If any of these resonates with you, then you are definitely at the right place. Keep reading to find out how I can help you!

And if you are ready to take the leap, let’s book a call and explore solutions for your family.
