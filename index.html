Hey there,

I'm Sophie,

your dyslexia coach !

And just like you, I'm a mom, parent of two wonderful dyslexic children, and proud dyslexic myself!

I aim to EMPOWER, to TEACH, and INSPIRE parents like me on their dyslexia journey through life experience , both as a dyslexic and as a mom of dyslexic children.

After homeschooling my children for several years, after learning how to better support each and everyone of them, and after helping them being thriving children, I wanted to help YOU - parents of dyslexic children - to do the same successfully.
